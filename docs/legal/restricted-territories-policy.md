# Restricted Territories Policy (DRAFT)

> **Status:** DRAFT — Pending counsel review. Do not rely for compliance decisions.

---

## Purpose

This policy defines jurisdictions where we **do not** offer services, accept users, or solicit. It will be updated based on legal counsel advice and regulatory changes.

---

## Default Restricted Territories (Provisional)

The following are **provisionally** restricted until counsel confirms or amends:

- **United States** (except where explicitly permitted by CFTC or state law; Kalshi/Polymarket have their own US frameworks)
- **United Kingdom** (FCA-regulated; separate licensing likely required)
- **European Union** (MiFID/gambling directives vary by member state)
- **Australia** (gambling and financial services regulations)
- **Canada** (provincial gambling laws)
- **Singapore, South Korea, China** (strict capital controls and/or gambling prohibitions)
- **Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea, Cuba** (OFAC and international sanctions)
- **Any jurisdiction** where prediction markets or event contracts are explicitly prohibited

---

## Implementation

- **Geo-blocking:** IP-based and (when available) KYC-based checks
- **Terms of Service:** Users must confirm they are not in a restricted territory
- **Ongoing review:** Quarterly review with counsel; update policy and systems accordingly
