# Bug Bounty Policy

**Status:** Draft. Launch after mainnet.

## Scope

- Smart contracts (Collateral, Market, Oracle)
- Critical: loss of funds, privilege escalation
- High: bypass of access control, oracle manipulation

## Out of Scope

- Frontend-only issues

- Third-party dependencies
- Social engineering
- Issues in testnet

## Rewards (Proposed)

| Severity | Bounty |
|----------|--------|
| Critical | $10,000 – $50,000 |
| High | $2,000 – $10,000 |
| Medium | $500 – $2,000 |
| Low | $100 – $500 |

## Process

1. Report to security@quantumbets.com (or equivalent)
2. Allow 90 days for remediation before public disclosure
3. No legal action for good-faith researchers
4. KYC may be required for payout

Consider Immunefi or Code4rena for structured program.
